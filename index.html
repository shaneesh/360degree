Mission

360 Degree Therapy - A level ahead in Healthcare 360 Degree Therapy is Committed to be a leading nationwide provider of healthcare solutions focusing on school-based therapy, Hospitals, Rehabilitation centers and early-intervention services. We aspire to change the way highly-qualified Speech-language Pathologists (SLPs), Occupational Therapists (OTs) and Physical Therapists (PTs.) perceive their profession. Welcome to the next level of professional achievement • Our leading school-based and early intervention programs are customized to meet the specific needs of our County/district partners in speech-language pathology, occupational therapy, and physical therapy. • 360 Degree Therapy clinicians use evidence-based practices. Our therapists implement assessment and intervention programs linked to educational standards, relevant to students' classroom needs, and focused on positive student outcomes

Vision

Our Vision is to be a leader in healthcare, education and innovation. Our meaning of leadership is being world class in our mission of healthcare, education, and research as well as being a rewarding and desirable place to work. This requires the pursuit of excellence in everything we do, being true to our values, collaboration among ourselves and with others, and maintaining fiscal responsibility.
